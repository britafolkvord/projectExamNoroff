Is SpaceX a branch of NASA?

SpaceX is a private company founded in 2002 by Elon Musk. We have secured over 100 missions to our manifest, these include commercial satellite launches as well as US government missions.

Where is SpaceX located?

We have development and launch facilities as well as offices across the US, but our headquarters is located in California.

How powerful is the Falcon Heavy rocket?

The Falcon Heavy rocket is the world's most powerful rocket. With more than 5 million pounds of thrust at liftoff, it equals approximately eighteen 747 aircrafts at full power.

Where does SpaceX test their rockets?

SpaceX tests its engines, vehicle structures, and systems at a 4,000-acre state-of-the-art rocket development facility in McGregor, Texas.
